Rui Qian (钱锐)

I am a junior undergraduate at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, major in Information Engineering.

At SJTU, I have been a member in MIN Lab, where I am advised by Prof. Weiyao Lin. And I am also honorable to work with Dr. Di Hu.

I am interested in computer vision and machine learning. I have done some research on spatio-temporal action localization, joint audio-visual learning, self-supervised learning for video understanding. Currently, I would like to try generative modelling and interpretable machine learning.

I am looking forward to pursuing a Ph.D. degree on computer vision and machine learning.
